⟡

Select two spells and fuse them. The oracle rules on what the fusion means — name, element, effect tags, flavor, and a visual signature chosen from a fixed vocabulary. It has no say over numbers: tier is fixed by recipe math (two equal tiers ascend, unequal tiers keep the higher), potency and cost are priced by a deterministic compiler, and the particle budget is capped by the engine, not the oracle.

Every ruling is canon: the first answer for a pair is cached and returned forever after, free. New discoveries refund +2 ✦.
