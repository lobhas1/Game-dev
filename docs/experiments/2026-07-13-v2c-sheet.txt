1. Beacon
   A: A fire that says: here, come here, look here.
   B: A steady flame guides the lost back to the path.
2. Blaze
   A: Wind breathed life into a coal; now nothing stands.
   B: A coal fed a breath, now running loose across dry grass.
3. Cairn
   A: Stones stacked where no one should pass, but might.
   B: Stones that remember where the darkness chose to rest.
4. Dust Devil
   A: A spinning skirt of grit that scours bare stone clean.
   B: The desert's oldest argument with the wind.
5. Lighthouse
   A: Steady beam guides lost travelers to safe ground.
   B: Steady beam cuts fog; ships find shore, storms find focus.
6. Mist
   A: Tiny droplets drift where the wind scatters them thin.
   B: Soft as forgetting, it settles where the wound waits.
7. Murk
   A: Still water gone dark, holding whatever sank into it.
   B: The world goes gray before you know you're lost.
8. Penumbra
   A: Soft gray where light and dark negotiate every edge.
   B: The edge where seeing ends and hiding begins.
9. Smoke
   A: Thick coils choke the air and swallow every outline.
   B: It hides what burned and blurs what stands.
10. Steam
   A: Scalding cloud that blurs the line between rain and ruin.
   B: Scalding cloud billows where cold water meets hot coal.
